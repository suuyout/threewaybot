Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 7, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled by the Discord bot ("Bot").

1. Data Collection

The Bot does not collect or store any personal user data. Some command usage may be logged temporarily to provide functionality.

2. Data Usage

Any collected data is used solely to provide bot functionality and is not shared with third parties.

3. Data Storage

Minimal non-personal data (like server or channel IDs) may be stored temporarily to maintain features like settings or moderation logs.

4. Your Rights

You may request deletion of any stored data related to your server by contacting the bot developer.

5. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the Bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
